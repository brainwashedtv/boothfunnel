Common questions

What people ask before they sign up.

How long does setup actually take?

Most venues are live in 15–25 minutes. The booth ships pre-configured to your brand; the only on-site steps are unbox, plug in, connect to Wi-Fi, and scan the QR to claim it.

What's actually included in the monthly fee?

The booth hardware (rented, swapped if it ever fails), unlimited digital shares, branded print credits per the plan, the analytics dashboard, on-call support, and free shipping both directions if you cancel.

What if my customers don't want to share?

Sharing is optional and fully consent-based. The booth still serves as a fun thing in your venue, and the print frame still carries your brand to wherever the photo ends up — fridge, scrapbook, or feed.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. Month-to-month, cancel any time, pay for the month you're in. We send a return label, you ship the booth back. You keep all your photos and analytics.

Do you handle the legal side of customer photos?

Yes. Customers consent to capture and share at the booth itself. We're the data processor on your behalf and provide a DPA so your privacy policy stays clean. Photos are deleted on a rolling 90-day window unless you change the setting.
